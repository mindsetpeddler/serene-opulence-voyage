**Project:** VedaVoyage - Premium Wellness & Travel Landing Page

**Theme:** Serene Opulence

**Color Palette:**
*   Primary: Deep Zen Charcoal (#121212), Soft Slate
*   Accents: Ethereal Gold (#C5A059), Copper

**Typography:**
*   Headings: Majestic, high-contrast Serif (e.g., Cormorant Garamond)
*   Body Text: Clean, wide-tracked Sans-Serif (e.g., Montserrat, Inter)

**Layout & Visual Effects:**

1.  **Hero Section:**
    *   Full-screen cinematic background (misty mountains/yoga shala, golden-hour lighting).
    *   Central text with subtle 'glow' effect.
    *   Transparent, minimalist navigation bar.
2.  **Reflects & Lighting:**
    *   'Specularity' on images and buttons (soft, polished sheen).
    *   'God rays' or soft light filtering background gradients.
    *   High-key gloss on images (professionally color-graded with crushed blacks and warm highlights).
3.  **Shapes & Containers:**
    *   Mix of 'Organic Geometric' shapes.
    *   Structured grid layout.
    *   Large circular masks for featured images (meditator, spa).
    *   'Glassmorphism' cards for testimonials/retreat details (semi-transparent, blurred backgrounds).
4.  **Interactions:**
    *   Smooth parallax scrolling with high-resolution texture imagery (silk, stone, incense smoke).
    *   'Ghost' buttons with thin gold borders that transition to a solid gold glow on hover.
5.  **Sections:**
    *   **Sacred Destinations Carousel:** High-gloss imagery.
    *   **Our Philosophy Section:** Heavy negative space for a breathable UX.
    *   **Book Your Journey Footer:** Rich, dark gradient, anchoring the page.
